Spring is a time of celebration (celebrating how we're no longer freezing). People think of Spring to last from March til the end of May. This is the time where Snoopy goes out and tosses eggs left and right. Baseball starts at this time while College basketball ends and NBA is in the middle of the season.Holidays associated with this holiday include Easter and leprechaun's day.
